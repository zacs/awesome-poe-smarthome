# awesome-poe-smarthome
A list of smarthome devices designed to be PoE-first. In general, these are devices that work well (or at all) with Home Assistant. 

## Cameras
Cameras on PoE is very standard, so this list should only be cameras that include extra sensors.

| Camera | Extra Sensors |
|---|---|
| Unifi G4/G5/AI | Glass break and smoke detector noise sensors |

## Air Quality

| Device | Sensors |
|---|---|
| Awair Omni | Temp, Humidity, PM2.5, tVOC, Light, Noise |
| Room Alert 3E | Temp, Humidity |

## Presence

| Device | Sensors |
|---|---|
| Apollo R-Pro 1 | mmWave, light, LED light |
| Everything Presence Pro | mmWave, PIR |

## Lighting

| Device | Description |
|---|---|
| Unifi Floodlight | LED floodlight with flush mount. Integrated motion sensor. |

## Sirens/Speakers

| Device | Capabilities |
|---|---|
